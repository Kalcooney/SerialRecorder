• What is this app?

  • SerialRecorder is an app that allows you to save and store serial numbers for your items. You can record the name, description, serial number, date of purchase and add an image for your item.
  • How to use this app?

  • Add Item:

    To add an item, firstly click the Add Item button in the Main Menu. Then:
    1. Enter in your item details by clicking on the input boxes.
    2. You can add an image by clicking on the camera button (left) or add the image from your gallery by clicking on the gallery button (right)
    3. Confirm your selection by clicking the add button (top right corner)

    View Items:

    To view your stored items, you only need to click on the View Items button in the Main Menu. This then brings up a list of all your stored entries and displays the item name, serial number for the item and the date that you purchased the item.
    To view the full entry of an item instead of the brief list view version, just click on the entry in the list and it will bring you to a full version view of that entry.

    Edit Items:

    To edit an entry for one of your items, do this:
    1. Click on View Items in Main Menu.
    2. Click on the item in the list that you would like to edit.
    3. Click on the three dot button (top right corner) and press edit.
    You are then brought to the Edit Item screen. From here you can update your item information simply by clicking on the fields and changing the information.
    To confirm your selection, simply click the add button (top right corner)
